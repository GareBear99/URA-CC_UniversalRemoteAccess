# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want URA-CC development to stay constructive, direct, and technically useful.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and specific
- criticize code and decisions, not people
- explain tradeoffs clearly
- avoid harassment, hostility, and personal attacks
- keep discussion grounded in product, architecture, and implementation quality

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject behavior that damages collaboration or makes the project harder to work on responsibly.
